What is sourdoughbake?

Sourdoughbake was founded on the idea of making tools to help a baker to improve and refine their skills. Our goal is to create baker tools we want to use. And design them with you in mind.

Making a loaf of bread should not be stressful or complicated. It should be a labor of love. We want to give you tools and share our experiences with you to help you on your path to sourdough magic. Regardless if this is your first or 500th loaf of bread, we are here to educate and inspire you on your baking journey.

The software is in early beta

There will be bugs

Sourdoughbake may still be in it's infancy, but we are dedicated to building you a full suite of tools. In the future, you can expect to see more bakers tools designed to help plan your ingredients, build timelines for your bakes, and to convert existing recipes from cups to weight.

Additionally, we have many topics for blog posts, recipes for things to do with your extra starter, recipes for things to put in or on your bread, and much more.
